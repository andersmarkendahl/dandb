Search

Empty string to fetch whole database.

Text must be enclosed with apostrophes ('').

Operands AND, OR and NOT are supported.

Examples:

  • lastName='Eriksson'
  • firstName='Per' OR firstName='Pehr'
  • firstName='Ingrid' OR (lastName='Eriksson' AND NOT gender='F')
